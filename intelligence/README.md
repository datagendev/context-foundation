# Intelligence

Recurring monitoring of external signals for intent detection and personalization.

## Files

### news-monitoring.md
Monitor external signals for prospect engagement:
- News and press releases
- PR mentions and announcements
- Reddit discussions
- LinkedIn posts and updates
- Industry events and conferences

Use case: Identify prospects who are hiring, expanding, or in the news.

### dashboard-setup.md
How to build dashboards on top of any folder:
- Real-time alerts when prospects appear in news
- Dashboard showing prospect activity scores
- Intelligence feeds for personalization
- Intent signals for lead scoring

## Intelligence Workflow

1. **Set up monitoring** for your ICP (see `/icp/`)
2. **Pull recurring intel** from multiple sources
3. **Score leads** based on intelligence signals
4. **Feed to dashboards** for visibility
5. **Use for personalization** in outreach (see `/outreach/`)
6. **Track in CRM** (see `/crm/`)

## Integration Points

- **Lead enrichment**: Add intelligence signals to CRM records
- **Campaign targeting**: Focus on high-intent prospects
- **Dashboard KPIs**: Track velocity of intent signals
- **Playbook context**: Use news/updates for email personalization
