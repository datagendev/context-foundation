# Pixis.ai - AI Marketing Platform

## Overview

Pixis is a complete, AI-powered advertising solution helping consumer brands improve ad performance and streamline workflows by bringing together the best of performance and creativity.

## Main Purpose

Pixis operates as an end-to-end AI advertising platform built on three core pillars:

### 1. Targeting
- AI-powered audience identification and conversion
- Optimized for specific brands, products, or ad campaigns
- Advanced audience segmentation capabilities

### 2. Creative
- Automated asset generation (text, images, emails)
- High-impact creativity tools via "Adroom" product
- On-brand creative production with reduced manual effort

### 3. Performance
- "Prism" - AI-powered command center for campaign performance
- Automated ad bid and budget pacing
- Real-time optimization to mitigate short-term fluctuations and seasonality
- Analytics trained on billions of data points
