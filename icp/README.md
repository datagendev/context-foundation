# ICP - Ideal Customer Profile

Define and refine your target market to focus your prospecting efforts.

## Files

### personas.md
Customer personas with detailed profiles:
- Pain points and challenges
- Budget and buying power
- Decision-making processes
- Industry and company size
- Real examples of ideal customers

### company-info-template.md
Template for describing your own company:
- Product/service overview
- Company story and positioning
- Unique value propositions
- Target industries
- OKRs and business metrics

## How to Use

1. **Define your ICP** in `personas.md`
   - Who is your ideal customer?
   - What problems do they have?
   - How do you reach them?

2. **Document your company** in `company-info-template.md`
   - This helps with personalization
   - Used for Llama Index context (see `/playbooks/`)
   - Referenced in outreach campaigns

3. **Link to TAM sources**
   - Use ICP to filter leads from `/TAM/`
   - Search LinkedIn for matching profiles
   - Web scrape for intent signals

4. **Use for campaign targeting**
   - Target specific industries in `/outreach/`
   - Configure dashboards (see `/dashboards/`) for ICP cohorts
